Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 400g boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (optional for thickening the sauce)
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. If you prefer a thicker sauce, add cornstarch to the mixture.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and ginger to the hot oil and stir-fry for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add chicken strips and cook until they are no longer pink.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. Stir-fry for another minute or until the sauce thickens and coats everything.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot over cooked rice.
