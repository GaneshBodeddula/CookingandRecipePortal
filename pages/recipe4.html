Grilled Salmon with Lemon Butter

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush both sides of the salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Place salmon fillets on the grill, along with lemon slices. Grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the salmon is cooked through and has grill marks.
  4. While the salmon is grilling, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and dried dill. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Squeeze grilled lemon slices into the melted butter mixture, and stir well.
  6. Remove salmon from the grill and drizzle with the lemon butter sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with additional lemon slices and dill, if desired.
