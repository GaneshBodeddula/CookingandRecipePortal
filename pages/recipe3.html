Vegetable Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, mushrooms)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, half of the mozzarella cheese, half of the Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, dried basil, and dried oregano.
  2. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce.
  3. Place three cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  4. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  5. Add half of the mixed vegetables on top of the ricotta mixture.
  6. Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and vegetables.
  7. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
